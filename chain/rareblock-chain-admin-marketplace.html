Reconciliation

Marketplace operations.

Match incoming bank transfers, trigger on-chain settlements, and manage fee configuration. All actions are logged and audit-tracked.

Bank transfers awaiting confirmation

When you see the buyer's bank transfer arrive on the bank statement, match it by the reference (causale) and click Mark paid. This will mark the order paid and automatically trigger the on-chain settlement.

Orders ready to settle on-chain

Orders in this list have been paid but the on-chain transfer hasn't completed (usually a transient RPC issue). Click Run settlement to execute the on-chain transfer manually.

Fee configuration

Buyer's premium varies by payment method (Sotheby's-style). Changes apply only to new orders — existing orders carry a frozen snapshot of fees from the moment they were created.
