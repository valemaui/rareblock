RAREBLOCK · PROMPT 02 — VAULT INTERIOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Uso: sezione "Custodia" (Sez. III del memorandum), full-bleed o tile
Aspect ratio target: 4:5 verticale → 1024×1280  (default del workflow)

─── PROMPT POSITIVO ───────────────────────────────────────────────

private archive vault interior, rows of identical unmarked black archival boxes neatly aligned on dark walnut wooden shelving with brass details, single warm directional tungsten lighting from a vintage brass sconce mounted on wall, deep dramatic shadows in foreground and corners, polished dark concrete floor with subtle reflection of the warm light, cinematic composition with shelves receding into darkness toward the back of frame, no labels visible on boxes, no logos, no text, museum-grade private storage facility, high-end safe deposit room, the kind of place a private collector keeps what truly matters, shot on medium format Hasselblad, Kodak Portra 800 film emulation, fine analog grain, gold accents only on metal hardware and lighting fixtures, deep sepia and warm black palette, editorial photography style, atmospheric depth, contemplative silence, suggestion of permanence and care


─── PROMPT NEGATIVO ───────────────────────────────────────────────

(VUOTO — Flux non usa negative)


─── VARIAZIONI DA PROVARE ─────────────────────────────────────────

A) Variante "key shot" — più simbolica, primo piano:
   Sostituisci tutto con:
   "extreme close-up macro shot of a single antique brass key resting on
   aged dark leather surface, single warm golden spotlight from upper-right
   corner, deep velvet shadow on left side, shallow depth of field f/2.8,
   intricate engraving on the key bow visible in sharp focus, dust motes
   floating in the light beam, evocative of safe deposit and private custody,
   museum catalog still life photography, gold and warm black palette only,
   shot on Phase One, fine grain, editorial luxury aesthetic"

B) Variante "ledger" — più documentale:
   Sostituisci tutto con:
   "still life of an open leather-bound ledger book on a dark walnut desk,
   handwritten entries visible but not readable, single brass desk lamp
   casting warm circle of light, pen and brass paperweight beside it,
   deep shadows around the lit area, archival aesthetic, private banker's
   office atmosphere, shot on Hasselblad, Portra 800 emulation, no faces,
   gold and warm black palette, editorial photography"

C) Se la prospettiva è piatta e vuoi più drama:
   Aggiungi: "low angle shot, three-point perspective, depth compression
   from medium format wide lens"
